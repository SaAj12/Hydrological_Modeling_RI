2010
================================================================================

1. March 12–16, 2010 – MARCH NOR'EASTER (St. Patrick's Day Nor'easter)
   Type: Extratropical / Nor'easter
   States: RI, MA, CT
   Impact: >10 inches rain, severe flooding; winds to 70 mph; >1M power outages;
           9+ deaths; coastal flooding, beach erosion.

2. September 4, 2010 – HURRICANE EARL
   Type: Hurricane (offshore)
   States: MA (Cape Cod, Nantucket, Martha's Vineyard)
   Impact: Passed ~90 mi offshore; heavy rain, large waves; tropical storm gusts
           (58 mph Hyannis); sustained 29–35 mph.

2011
================================================================================

3. August 28, 2011 – HURRICANE IRENE
   Type: Hurricane → Tropical Storm (landfall NY, then into New England)
   States: RI, MA, CT
   Impact: 70 mph at landfall; major flooding (especially western New England);
           hurricane-force gusts Westport–Woods Hole; 16+ deaths in New England
           (10 in CT); historic Vermont flooding.

    States: RI, MA
